Class: RoomPlansFactory

RoomPlansFactory()

new RoomPlansFactory()

Factory for creating room plans. Handles the planning and placement of structures, roads, and ramparts. Provides methods for initial room setup and later new room planning, including source mining, mineral extraction, base construction, and plant construction.
